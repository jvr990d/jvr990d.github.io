FedEx

This website is for shipping, printing and designing products.

  • Contrast: This site has great contrast the background color is white, while the images and text are bright colors like orange, blue and black.
  • Repetion: FedEx makes great use of repetion in that to the right of every image has its own description with a header, paragraph, and finally a link.
  • Alignment: Alignment could use a little work in that everything is really just center align, though this does add a sence of simplicity and oragnaization.
  • Proximity: Similar to Repetion they made sure that directly following an image they have an explanation near.

Craigslist

This is a classified web page where local people can sell shop and get scammed.

  • Contrast: This page has good but basic contrast with a white background and blue text.
  • Repetion: This page has good Repetition but again basic in that it is just a header followed by a column.
  • Allignment: Header is centered and words are right allign, this is very basic.
  • Proximity: No real proxitmity as it all looks like a giant list.
